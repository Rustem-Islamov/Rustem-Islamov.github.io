Building Advanced Learning Algorithms and AI Systems

Through rigorous theoretical foundations and principled algorithm design

My research explores the overlap between practical machine-learning methods and their underlying theory — a space where recent progress in training deep networks still depends largely on heuristics and has little theoretical backing. In my research, I aim to narrow the gap between theory and practice by (1) developing practical theory that explains why specific phenomena arise and why certain heuristics succeed during deep-learning training, (2) advancing existing algorithms by theoretically inspired modifications, and (3) creating new methods with both strong convergence guarantees and practical performance.
