Clip21-SGD2M: new method with strong optimization and DP guarantees

Our new paper on distributed optimization with strong optimization and DP guarantees is out! We introduce Clip21-SGD2M, a method featuring a double momentum mechanism‚Äîone for managing stochastic noise and another for averaging DP noise.
